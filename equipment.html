Equipment Best Practices

  • fORP System: Please coil the button box cord and place it front left side of the scanner room. Make sure the buttons are face up.
  • Prescription Glasses: If mix and matching lenses to match someone's prescription, please do not mix up the lenses and the bag!
  • NNL Display System: Confirm with the participant that they can view the screen with the mirror system before placing them in the bore.

Emergency Procedures

For Participant Medical Emergencies:

  • Hit the red stop button on the scanner console to end any current scan
  • One staff member coordinates participant care, the other dials 911 and provides the following address: "675 Nelson Rising Ln, 4th St entrance"
  • Get the participant out of the scanner room and into the hallway ASAP

For Magnetic Emergency:

  • If the participant's life or limb is at stake, hit the quench button
  • If the participant is trapped but not at risk, do not hit the quench button

In the Event of Earthquake, Flood, Fire, or Electrical Arcing:

  • Hit the emergency power off
