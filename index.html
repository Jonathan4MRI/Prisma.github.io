Emergency Procedures

For Participant Medical Emergencies:

  • Hit the red stop button on the scanner console to end any current scan
  • One staff member coordinates participant care, the other dials 911 and provides the following address: "675 Nelson Rising Ln, 4th St entrance"
  • Get the participant out of the scanner room and into the hallway ASAP

For Magnetic Emergency:

  • If the participant's life or limb is at stake, hit the quench button
  • If the participant is trapped but not at risk, do not hit the quench button

In the Event of Earthquake, Flood, Fire, or Electrical Arcing:

  • Hit the emergency power off

üìÖ Neuroscape Imaging Core Scheduling Guidelines

1. Global Email Lists

The Prisma has two global email lists:

  • NIC_Active - used for scanner announcements
  • NIC_Cancel - used when a scheduled booking becomes available again

To get added to these Prisma's scheduling notification lists follow instructions at:
https://wiki.library.ucsf.edu/display/Neuro/Neuroscape+Scanner+Lists

‚ö† Important: Please send an email to NIC_CANCEL@listserv.ucsf.edu when you cancel bookings within 15 business days of the scheduled time so someone else may use that spot.

2. Booking Requirements

Do not schedule until you have a Participant lined up for the time slot you have selected or are ready to run a phantom. Holding spots without including participant specific anonymized ID in the Description while recruiting is not permitted.

3. Researcher Collaboration

It is appropriate to email other researchers and ask them if they can move a scan up or down the schedule slightly to make yours fit somewhere. Everyone tries to accommodate requests like that when possible.

4. Testing and Training Sessions

Scheduling Types labeled as Testing and Training should include one of those words in the Description section.

üí∞ Free Scans: These scans are provided at no cost. Because they are free, please schedule them either off hours or within 24 hours of the desired date/time.

If scheduled further in advance, please yield to a participant booking if needed.

üîí Privacy Notice

The above instructions are for scheduling on the Prisma's billing calendar. Do not include any ePHI, including initials, on this calendar as it is public.

üìç Note from NCRU

You or another team member are required to greet participants and their caregivers in the atrium of the building. Please be sure they have a way to reach you or another study member when they arrive.
